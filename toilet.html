Smart Toilet Display

Display the available status of the toilet at the airport for travelers


Project Flow

In this project, my role is as a UI designer. I am responsible for: a. Analyze Needs from clients, b. Collect Info and Observe, C.Interview, D.UI Design, and cooperate with engineers.


Research

Collect Info

Before designing, I collect some design cases of toilet display. There two kinds of designs: a. Display the single room's status, b. Show the toilet is accessible or not.



On site observation

Besides collecting data from the net, I also go to the airport and other public spaces to investigate the signs and display of toilets.


Draft

I show the draft to some users and ask for feedback about the design. I found that lots of them didn't care about the rooms' status (occupied or not ). And it's challenging for users to figure out the current situation.




Reflection

Could users still remember which room is unoccupied when they are entering the toilet? Or shows the number of empty rooms, then they will find it out.

What can users do? When they found all the rooms were occupied. Are there other toilets near here?


Final Design
