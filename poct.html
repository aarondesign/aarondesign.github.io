Point of Care Test Project

Point of care test project include a portable device and web platforms. The object of this product is to measure the patients’ lipid and Alc level in the blood. In this project, my job is design the app and web system including flows and user interface.

Not only design the product, I also go to hospital to observe and interview with some patients. Then report some usability issues to the managers and give some suggestions about how to improve the product.

User's Journey

Before designing the UI flows, the first thing is to figure out the scenario that this product will be used by what kind of people and what kind of benefit we can provide.

In this project, healthcare center will buy this product to measure the customs’ blood. According the test result, the system will give custom health advices, health supplements and the test records.

After some months, system will remind customs to come back for follow up at healthcare center.
