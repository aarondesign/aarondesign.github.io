Luteinizing Hormone Test Project

Using the phone camera to read the test strip that absorbs the woman's urine, then the application will show the level of luteinizing hormone to info user when is the best fertile window.

In this project, I am responsible for designing the user flow and interface. I also introduced the usability test and users' interview in our organization. In the result, the users' voice entered our meeting room, and we improve some of the issues.

It's a challenge for me ( as a male ) to design a fertility test product for females.

User's Journey

A woman who prepares for pregnancy will try kinds of method. One of that is testing the LH level in the urine. In this project, the users will buy the test strip from the pharmacies in the first step. Before test the urine, they should download the application then input the period data to predict the next cycle. When the test day arrives, the app will push notification to info use doing the test.

The user takes a test strip, immersing in the urine and wait for minutes until the test strip reacted. After that, the user will clip the test strip on the phone to analyze the test strip. In a few seconds, it will show the LH level, peak, high and low. The Peak and high level mean it's an excellent fertile day, do the right things now. Repeat the process in a few months, and the users will welcome a new family member.

Design Concept

The main job on this application is to measure the test strip. I want to let user aware when the measurement process starts naturally. Therefore I use two kinds of background to imply user the status. The grey one shows on the settings page, the calendar page and the onboarding; The dark one used on the measurement process.

Usability Test

Before releasing the formal version, I will find some test in our organization.
Before the test, I will tell the testees about the scenario and allocate missions to them and request the testees to think aloud so that I can know what they think currently. I will record the behaviors during the test, and then using the matrix to find out the issues/problems during the usability test. In the result, I will plan kinds of design to solve the problems.

Design Review

Designing the flow to guide users to place the strip on the correct area is one of the challenge parts. After the first version released, there are some terrible feedbacks about putting the test strip. I hold the product test to find out some usability issues. In the result, the complaint about placing the test strip is rooted out.

What I learn from this project

1⃣ Learn from users:
Users are our teachers. Before redesigning the product, our team interviewed some women preparing pregnancy for months, trying different methods. They shared us about the stories, the pain points, what they experienced, and the way how they use products for pregnancy. To browse internet forums is another way to understand users. They will share the whole stories in every detail about preparing pregnancy selflessly, and the most important things for us, that is FREE.


2️⃣ Test, test and test:
I host some usability test in this project. I will set a scenario and give testers missions to achieve. During the test, I record the time testers cost and problems what they had. At the end of the test, I would ask them why they operated that way and request them to fill the SUS ( System Usability Scale ) meters survey. I improve the design according to what I discovered lots of things during the test.
